    Toro Loco makes the best margaritas.

    You don't have to be in Mexico to enjoy mango, kiwi, and banana daiquiris; sizzling fajitas; chimichangas; red snapper seasoned with Mexican spices, pepper, onions, tomatoes and mushrooms; shrimp simmered in a creamy pumpkin seed sauce. Tenured Chef Ignacio Guzman prepares everything from scratch in this very festive, family operated Mexican restaurant. On Fridays and Saturdays, two guitarists and a singer fill the air with the sound of Mexican music as they stroll between tables.

    Our brand new menu includes:

    • New, delicious Mexican dishes.
    • New desserts.
    • New cocktails incorporating smoky Mexican mezcal and smooth tequila.

    We have been in South Orange for more than 30 years. bringing the best of Mexico and, most importantly, our friendly service.

    Why choose Toro Loco Our Restaurant
